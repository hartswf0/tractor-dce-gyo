Interfaces â€“ how Momento talks to Grace
MomentoInterface (Grace)
Defined in wag-grace-editor.html. After the Grace viewer initializes, it sets window.MomentoInterface = { renderer, scene, camera, renderCurrentView(...) } and posts a GRACE_SCENE_READY message to the parent window.
Handshake & Status
In momento_capture_app.js, Momento listens for GRACE_SCENE_READY from the iframe, caches a reference to iframe.contentWindow.MomentoInterface, and logs readiness before enabling capture controls.
Capture Contract
Momento assumes: (1) renderer.domElement is a WebGL canvas, (2) renderCurrentView() draws one frame, and (3) the camera can be safely animated between frames without breaking Grace.
