SWISS DESIGNATOR
Top-level hub. Lets you pick builders (-ators), open docs, and jump into worlds. Shows status (FRANK online/offline) and acts as a launcher for all flows.
WAG FRANK
Integrator + composer on the wag-frank bus. Collects MPDs from all -ators (and future builders like Thousand Tetrad), sketches a grid layout, and exports wag-frank-scene.mpd with FRANK_CELL metadata.
GRACE EDITOR / VIEWER
Stage + MPD text editor. Listens on wag-frank for scene-mpd, renders the 3D world, and lets you edit the MPD directly. Can host Swiss / Frank panels side-by-side.

Builders as Lego blocks
SCENEERATOR
Picked first. Chooses a big SCENE SHELL (crater, road+river, raised island, ship hull). Broadcasts scene-shell-context + scene-mpd.
LOCATIONATOR
Builds production sets on top of the shell. Uses taxonomy + location-library for FLOOR/WALL/DOOR/WINDOW/ROOF/TREE/PROP.
VEHICULATOR
Builds vehicles using taxonomy-driven slots (chassis, wheels, windscreens, hulls, engines) and exports vehicle_mpd + fleet scenes.
MINIFIGURATOR
Builds characters (single + families). Outputs minifig-mpd + family MPDs to place into locations and scenes.
GRACE / WAG VIEWER
Receives MPDs + specs over wag-frank. Renders the world, loads parts from taxonomy, and acts as the stage.

Flow 3 ‚Äì Taxonomy & primitives underneath
parts-taxonomy.json
Hierarchical tree of kingdoms ‚Üí families ‚Üí parts. Used by Locationator + Vehiculator (and Grace catalog) to pull real parts.
taxonomy-primitives.json
Bottom-up primitive heads (wheel, windscreen, door, panel, baseplate...). Drives slot buckets and new -ators.
scene-shells.json
Filtered, high-geometry baseplates & hulls. Sceneerator uses this as the catalog of big ready-made worlds.
