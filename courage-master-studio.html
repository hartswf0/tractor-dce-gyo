STUDIO – COURAGE / MASTER

Use the tabs to move between Courage (authoritative GOLD snapshot producer) and WAG Master (skeleton-aware MPD editor) without leaving this window.

  • Courage – load MPD scenes and emit GOLD snapshots with mpd_content, stud_skeleton, part_skeletons, and ground_violations.
  • WAG Master – load MPD or GOLD JSON, view bones, studs, and per-part control dots, and export edited MPD.

TABS shows one tool at a time. 2-UP is for large screens – Courage and WAG Master side by side.
