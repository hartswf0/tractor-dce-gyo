TIMBER – Integrated Studio

TIMBER hosts Courage (authoring), WERE (skeleton inspector), Master (room-scale composer), and Symbiogene (skeleton lab) in a single shared studio, all wired together over the same GOLD + selection bus.

  • Courage – load and edit MPD scenes, then emit GOLD snapshots with mpd_content, stud_skeleton, part_skeletons, and ground_violations.
  • WERE – inspect GOLD skeletons as per-part/per-layer bones and ORBS, and export edited MPD back to Courage.
  • Master – explore the same GOLD in a bounded "room", visualize collisions, envelopes, and control dots.
  • Symbiogene – experiment with raw GOLD structures (stud clouds, violations) for calibration and debugging.

TABS shows one instrument at a time. 3-UP is for large screens – Courage, WERE, and Master side by side with horizontal scroll when needed.

Core workflows (actually wired today):

  • GOLD loop – in Courage, load/paste an MPD and emit a GOLD snapshot. TIMBER forwards that GOLD to WERE and Master, and forwards just the stud_skeleton to Symbiogene.
  • Selection loop – in WERE, click a bone or ORB cluster. WERE sends a selection event up to TIMBER, which highlights the same parts in Courage (by line number) and in Master (by bones/cubes).
  • Skeleton debug loop – after emitting GOLD, switch to Symbiogene to inspect the raw stud cloud and violations coming from the same snapshot before or after using WERE/Master.
