STUDIO – SWISS / FRANK / GRACE

Use the tabs to switch between Swiss (hub), Frank (bus/composer), and Grace (stage) without leaving this window.

  • Swiss – pick designers and flows.
  • Frank – collect minifigs/locations/vehicles on a 9×9 grid.
  • Grace – render and edit the combined MPD scene.

TABS mode shows one tool at a time. 3-UP is for large screens – all three side by side with scroll.
